About Me

I am a student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign pursuing a BS in Computer Science + Economics, minoring in Music. I am a highly-motivated learner and am passionate about ML/AI, audio signal processing, quantitative finance, and applying computation and logic to various kinds of problem solving. I am also a classically trained violinist, having studied Western and South Indian Carnatic music for fourteen years. I am currently studying electric violin with Professor Rudolf Haken, for which I have been using various DAWs, MIDI sequencers, and other audio software to produce backing tracks, loops, and original compositions.
